How we get the numbers

🎰 Hard Rock +EV bets

We take the lines at sharp sportsbooks (Pinnacle and peers), strip out the bookmaker's margin to estimate the true probability, then compare that fair value to Hard Rock's price. When Hard Rock pays more than the bet is truly worth, the gap is the edge (+EV). Stake suggestions use quarter-Kelly, and an Elo cross-check flags moneylines where our model disagrees with the market.

⚽ World Cup predictions

Team strengths come from the current standings and form, blended with live market odds when available, and feed a goal-scoring model that turns into win / total / both-teams-to-score / handicap probabilities. Who advances is a 20,000-run Monte Carlo over the remaining group matches using FIFA tiebreakers.
